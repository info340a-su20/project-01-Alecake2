Project Topic Proposal

Problem Description

Students will perfom better in their academics if they can find courses that matches their learning styles. However, they sometimes don't get enough accurate information about their classes before they design their schedules. One of the most common way for getting information about a course and instructor is through RateMyProfessor.com. Which is kind of bias sometimes, and teachers don't get the chance to introduce themselves. Also, the rating score can be skewed by extreme scores when the number of students who rate one instructor is small. For more information on the disadvantage of this website, please read article Opinion: Students should not solely rely on Rate My Professors to pick classes.

Proposed Application

The website I have a in my mind contains a new system that allows UW students to get better insights about the class they are interested taking. When one professor sets up a profile for one of his courses, he/she get to write an introduction on his teaching style. Then when students rate and comment the professor, they will give a rate base on how accurate is this professor's description is rather than on how good is this instuctor. In addition, each student can put the average number of hours they spend on this class. On the other hand, the instuctors can put more information on their page such as sample lecture video, average grade, average reading load and average number of practice problems.

By collecting and presenting more factual data like data from previous quarters rather than students' opinions, UW students can gather more accurate information on whether the style of one course fits their learning habits when they search the course, and combine the description of the instructors and students' rating can make the result less biased. Also, being able to watch a sample lecture can be very helpful on assessing whether this class fits students' learning style.
